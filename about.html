Company Profile

Pune's Most Trusted Corrugated Box Manufacturer

At Shivam Cartons Pvt. Ltd., we believe packaging is more than protection—it is a promise of quality, reliability, and innovation. As a trusted partner to leading industries across India, we specialize in manufacturing high-performance corrugated cartons that meet the most demanding standards of strength, sustainability, and precision. Established in 1999 under the leadership of Mr. Sachin Gore, with extensive experience in corrugated packaging, Shivam Cartons Pvt. Ltd. has consistently delivered excellence in packaging solutions. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified and proud members of FCBM/WICMA.

Mission & Values

What Drives Us Every Day

Our Mission

Deliver cartons that don’t just carry products—they carry trust, safety, and value.

Our Vision

To be the preferred packaging partner for businesses worldwide by combining innovation, sustainability, and operational excellence.

Sustainability

100% recyclable kraft liners, water-based inks, FSC-compliant materials and a zero-waste-to-landfill plant policy — green packaging by design.

Customer First

Our dedicated teams, swift sampling processes, and adaptable MOQs ensure that every client engagement is treated as a strategic partnership rather than a simple transaction.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous in-house QA — Burst Factor, ECT, COBB, GSM and moisture tests at every stage of production ensure zero-compromise on quality.

In-House Production

Complete end-to-end manufacturing under one roof — from raw kraft paper to finished, bundled cartons ready for dispatch.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

1999

Foundation

Shivam Cartons Pvt. Ltd. founded by Mr. Sachin Gore with a vision to provide quality corrugated packaging to Pune's growing industries.

2005

Plant Expansion

Relocated and expanded to the Chandkhed, Maval facility — a 40,000 sq. ft. state-of-the-art manufacturing plant.

2010

Advance Technologies

Invested in advanced corrugation and flexo printing machinery, enhancing production capacity and quality consistency across all product lines.

2013

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001 certification, formalising our commitment to quality management and customer satisfaction.

2018

Capacity Milestone

Reached significant TPA production capacity milestones, onboarding marquee clients across FMCG and industrial sectors.

About Shivam Cartons

Pune's Most Trusted Corrugated Box Manufacturer

At Shivam Cartons Pvt. Ltd., we believe packaging is more than protection—it is a promise of quality, reliability, and innovation. As a trusted partner to leading industries across India, we specialize in manufacturing high-performance corrugated cartons that meet the most demanding standards of strength, sustainability, and precision. Established in 1999 under the leadership of Mr. Sachin Gore, with extensive experience in corrugated packaging, Shivam Cartons Pvt. Ltd. has consistently delivered excellence in packaging solutions. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified and proud members of FCBM/WICMA.

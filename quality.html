QA Process

Our 6-Stage Quality Control Process

1

Raw Material Inspection

Every roll of kraft paper and liner board is tested for GSM, moisture content and burst strength before entering production. Rejected rolls are returned to supplier.

2

Corrugation Stage Check

Flute height, glue penetration and board flatness are verified at the corrugator. Samples from each production run are tested for ECT and delamination resistance.

3

Print Quality Verification

Colour density, registration accuracy and ink adhesion are checked after every print run. PANTONE colour matching available for branded cartons on request.

4

Die-Cut & Crease Accuracy

Die-cut dimensions and crease positions are measured against approved drawings using digital callipers. Tolerances of ±1mm are maintained across all orders.

5

Finished Box Testing

Assembled boxes undergo Burst Factor, ECT, COBB and drop tests. Random samples from every batch are tested against the approved quality plan before dispatch.

6

Dispatch Verification

Each bundled consignment is verified for quantity, labelling and condition. A quality certificate is issued with every dispatch for traceability and customer records.

Policies & Compliance

Our Governance Framework

Quality Policy

  • Deliver defect-free products — First Time Right, Every Time
  • Meet or exceed customer specifications on every order
  • Continual improvement of QMS processes and KPIs
  • Employee training on quality standards and procedures
  • Supplier evaluation and material quality audits

Environmental Policy

  • Use of water-based, non-toxic flexographic inks only
  • FSC-compliant kraft liners from certified suppliers
  • Zero-waste-to-landfill plant policy — all scrap recycled
  • Energy-efficient machinery and LED plant lighting
  • Rainwater harvesting at the Chandkhed plant

Health & Safety Policy

  • Mandatory PPE — gloves, eye protection and ear guards

  • Zero tolerance for unsafe working conditions

HR & Labour Policy

  • Provident Fund (PF) and ESIC for all permanent employees
  • No child labour — verified age documentation required

  • Annual performance reviews and skill-development training
